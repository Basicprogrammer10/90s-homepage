The Best Season: Winter

My favorite season is winter because I love the cold weather, the snow, and the festivities that come with the holiday season. During the winter, I enjoy skiing, snowshoeing, and making snowmen with my family. My favorite winter memory is going on a family ski trip to Vermont - the snow was perfect, the slopes were challenging, and the hot cocoa was delicious!
